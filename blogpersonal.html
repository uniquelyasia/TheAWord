Who am I?

Hi there! For those of you who don’t know me, my name is Asia (yes, like the continent). I am a game designer, coder, honor roll student, co-founder of a non profit, volunteer, and tons of other impressive titles that would look great on a resume. However, I am most proud of being a young, black, female. Growing up as the youngest of four girls in a God-fearing house-hold, I’ve always appreciated expression. I feel as though art and expression take place in almost any form and journalism is my favorite one. I want to be able to share and express my thoughts, regardless of how big or little the topic may be. Thus, welcome to “The A Word”! Not just a blog but my personal form of expression. With that, I hope you can take time out of your day to stop by and read new entries. But of course, what would expression be without commentary? I want to hear your thoughts, answer your questions, and discuss topics that are important to YOU no matter how up close and personal they may be. Feel free to contact me and write on!
